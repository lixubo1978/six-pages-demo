SinoWellJourneys operates as a European travel organiser providing China cultural and wellness programs to licensed travel agencies in Hungary and across the European Union.

We do not sell directly to consumers. All customer sales and payments are handled by our authorised travel agency partners.

Our Legal Structure

SinoWellJourneys is operated by:

SinoWellJourneys Travel Platform Kft
Budapest, Hungary

The company acts as:

  • A travel organiser
  • A wholesale product platform
  • A cross-border service coordinator

How Our Business Works

Our operating model is fully transparent and contract-based.

  1. European travel agencies sell SinoWellJourneys programs to end customers.
  2. Travel agencies collect all customer payments.
  3. Travel agencies purchase the programs from SinoWellJourneys at agreed wholesale prices.
  4. SinoWellJourneys contracts and pays licensed Chinese service providers to deliver the services.

SinoWellJourneys never collects or holds consumer funds.

Our Service Providers

SinoWellJourneys works exclusively with:

  • Licensed Chinese travel agencies
  • Licensed Chinese medical and wellness centres
  • Contracted local service providers

All suppliers operate under formal service agreements and issue invoices for their services.

Medical & Wellness Services

SinoWellJourneys does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment. All medical and wellness services are delivered solely by licensed Chinese medical institutions in China, in accordance with Chinese law.

Payments & Anti-Money Laundering

All payments to Chinese service providers are made by SinoWellJourneys from Hungary under commercial service contracts and supported by:

  • Service agreements
  • Invoices
  • Travel program references
  • Customer and group records

This ensures full traceability, transparency and compliance with EU and international AML regulations.

Consumer Protection

End customers contract only with their chosen travel agency.

Travel agencies are responsible for:

  • Customer payments
  • Refunds and cancellations
  • Consumer protection and travel insurance

This ensures compliance with EU Package Travel and consumer protection law.

Why This Structure Matters

This structure ensures:

  • No consumer funds held by SinoWellJourneys
  • Clear separation of sales, organisation and service delivery
  • Fully auditable cross-border payments
  • Legal compliance in both the EU and China
