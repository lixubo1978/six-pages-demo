About SinoWellJourneys

Q: What is SinoWellJourneys?

SinoWellJourneys is an EU-based travel organiser that designs and coordinates China cultural and wellness travel programs for licensed travel agencies in Hungary and across Europe.

Q: Are you a travel agency?

No. SinoWellJourneys is a travel organiser and product platform. All customer sales and payments are handled by authorised retail travel agencies.

Booking & Payments

Q: Can I book directly with SinoWellJourneys?

No. For consumer protection and regulatory compliance, all bookings are made through licensed travel agencies.

Q: Who do I pay?

You pay your chosen travel agency. SinoWellJourneys does not collect payments from travellers.

Q: Why is payment required before travel?

China programs involve hotels, medical services and local suppliers that must be prepaid. This ensures your trip is properly secured and confirmed.

Programs & Services

Q: Who provides the travel services in China?

All travel services are provided by licensed Chinese travel agencies and local service providers contracted by SinoWellJourneys.

Q: Who provides the medical and wellness services?

All medical and wellness services are delivered by licensed Chinese medical institutions. SinoWellJourneys and travel agencies do not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

Q: Is medical insurance included?

Travel and medical insurance is arranged by your travel agency. We strongly recommend comprehensive travel and medical coverage.

Safety & Compliance

Q: Are your suppliers licensed?

Yes. All Chinese travel agencies and medical centres we work with are legally licensed in China.

Q: Is this compliant with EU law?

Yes. SinoWellJourneys operates as a travel organiser and works through licensed EU travel agencies who handle consumer protection and booking obligations.

Refunds & Complaints

Q: Who do I contact if something goes wrong?

You contact your travel agency. They handle customer service, refunds and complaints.

Q: Can SinoWellJourneys issue refunds?

No. Refunds are handled by the travel agency that sold you the trip.

For Travel Agencies

Q: How do I become a SinoWellJourneys partner?

If you are a licensed travel agency, contact us at info@sinowelljourneys.eu to receive our trade rates and program details.

For Banks & Partners

Q: Does SinoWellJourneys handle customer funds?

No. All customer funds are collected by retail travel agencies. SinoWellJourneys only receives B2B payments for organised services.

Q: How are payments to China made?

SinoWellJourneys pays licensed Chinese suppliers under formal service contracts and invoices, ensuring full transparency and compliance.
