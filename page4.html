SinoWellJourneys is an EU-based travel organiser dedicated to connecting European travellers with authentic Chinese cultural and wellness experiences. We operate as a professional product and programme platform, working with licensed travel agencies in Europe and licensed service providers in China.

Who We Are

SinoWellJourneys is operated by SinoWellJourneys Travel Platform Kft, a company registered in Budapest, Hungary.

We specialise in:

  • Designing China cultural and wellness travel programmes
  • Coordinating licensed travel and medical service providers
  • Supporting European travel agencies with high-quality long-haul products

We do not sell directly to travellers. All customer sales are handled by our authorised retail travel partners.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make China accessible, safe and inspiring for European travellers.

We believe that:

  • Cultural exchange builds understanding
  • Professional wellness and health services enhance travel
  • Strong partnerships create the best experiences

Our Network

SinoWellJourneys works with a growing network of:

  • Licensed Chinese travel agencies
  • Licensed Chinese medical and wellness centres
  • Hotels, transport providers and professional guides
  • European retail travel agencies

Each partner is selected based on legal compliance, quality standards and long-term reliability.

Our Standards

We apply strict standards across all programmes:

  • Licensed and audited suppliers
  • Contract-based service delivery
  • Clear pricing and transparent billing
  • Professional customer support via our agency partners

Our Role

SinoWellJourneys acts as:

  • A travel organiser
  • A wholesale product provider
  • A cross-border service coordinator

We do not act as:

  • A retail travel agency
  • An online booking platform
  • A medical provider

Our Location

SinoWellJourneys operates from Budapest, Hungary, serving travel agencies and travellers across Europe.
